How do you help somebody with depression?

  • Akriti Rathore
  • Web Design
  • 14

Depression is a common mental disorder. Globally, more than 300 million people of all ages suffer from depression.
People who have gone through adverse life events (unemployment, bereavement, psychological trauma) are more likely to develop depression. It can, in turn, lead to more stress and dysfunction and worsen the affected person’s life. At its worst, depression can lead to suicide.

What are the reason for Depression??

1. By Holding On to Past Hurts.

* If you refuse to deal with past hurts, then you might end up being depressed. Because it keeps your mind fixed on the bad experiences you have had in life.
Thus, causing you to constantly think on how undeserving you are of the good things of life. And the more you allow these negative thoughts in your mind, the more depressed you become.

2. Through Experiencing A Major Loss

* Either the loss of loved one, loss of job or a divorce. Any kind of loss that affect you immensely can eventually lead to depression.

3. By Having Unrealistic Expectations Of Yourself And Life.

4. Through Lack Of Adequate Self-Love.

* Not having enough self-love will make you lack compassion for yourself.

More women are affected by depression than men..

Tips To Help Someone With Depression??

Wondering What to say to Somebody with Depression....?? I Totally Agree When it come to Help Our Loved One it became more difficult. But there are some measures we can take to their recovery from their worst situation called "Depression":

1. Let that person know you care for them and you are there to listen them.

2. Accept them as they are, without judging them.

3. Stay in touch with them by messaging, texting, phoning or meeting for coffee. Try to Make them feel a bit special....I think by doing this you are not going to loose anything. Because people who are depressed become isolated and may find it difficult to leave their home.

4. Gently encourage them to help themselves – for example, by staying physically active, eating a balanced diet and doing things they enjoy.

5. Show all your concern for them. Treat them with Love and affection. I think Love is the most Powerful thing in this World it can solve all problem.
